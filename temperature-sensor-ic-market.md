<p><p>The Temperature Sensor IC market can be categorized into two types: Analog Temperature Sensor IC and Digital Temperature Sensor IC. </p><p>Analog Temperature Sensor ICs measure temperature through the generation of an analog voltage or current output proportional to the temperature. These sensors are suitable for applications where accurate temperature measurement is required.</p><p>On the other hand, Digital Temperature Sensor ICs convert temperature readings into digital outputs using internal circuitry. These sensors offer advantages such as high accuracy, low power consumption, and compatibility with digital systems.</p><p>Both types serve the purpose of measuring temperature but differ in terms of the output they provide and their specific use cases.</p></p>
