<p><p>The smart wireless smoke detector market includes two main types: ionic smoke alarms and photoelectric smoke alarms. Ionic smoke alarms use an ionization process that detects small particles released during a fire, while photoelectric smoke alarms use a beam of light to detect larger smoke particles. Both types are effective in detecting different types of fires, with ionic smoke alarms being more sensitive to flaming fires and photoelectric smoke alarms being more sensitive to smoldering fires. These smart wireless smoke detectors provide enhanced connectivity and alert features, ensuring improved safety and quicker response times.</p></p>
