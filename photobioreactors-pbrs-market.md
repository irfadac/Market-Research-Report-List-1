<p><strong>The Photobioreactors (PBRs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open systems</li><li>Closed systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photobioreactors (PBRs) are systems used for cultivating and harvesting microorganisms like algae or cyanobacteria. Open systems allow direct exposure to sunlight and atmospheric air, facilitating lower costs and higher biomass production but with limited control over environmental conditions. Closed systems, on the other hand, offer greater control over light, temperature, and nutrient availability, but incur higher costs due to the need for artificial lighting and air supply. The PBR market includes both open and closed systems, each catering to different needs and applications in sectors such as biofuels, wastewater treatment, and food supplements.</p></p>
