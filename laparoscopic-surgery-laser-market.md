<p><p>The Laparoscopic Surgery Laser Market is divided into various types, including CO2 Lasers, Argon Lasers, Nd:YAG Lasers, Diode Lasers, and Others. CO2 lasers are commonly used due to their high absorption by water, making them ideal for tissue cutting. Argon lasers produce a green light which is well-absorbed by red tissues, commonly used for coagulation. Nd:YAG lasers have deep tissue penetration and are used for cutting and coagulation. Diode lasers produce infrared light and are versatile for various applications. Other types include KTP, Holmium, and Thulium lasers, each with their own unique features and uses in laparoscopic surgery.</p></p>
