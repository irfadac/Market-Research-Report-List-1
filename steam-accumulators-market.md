<p><p>Steam accumulators are used in industries to store excess steam for later use. They come in two market types: vertical type and horizontal type. Vertical type accumulators have a vertical design, making them suitable for limited floor space and high-pressure applications. They are efficient and require less time for heating. Horizontal type accumulators, on the other hand, have a horizontal design and are commonly used in industries with ample floor space. They can handle large amounts of steam and provide continuous steam supply for longer periods.</p></p>
