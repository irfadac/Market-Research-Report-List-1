<p><p>Muffle furnaces are categorized into three types based on their operating temperature range. Low temperature muffle furnaces typically operate between 100°C and 1,100°C and are suitable for applications that require heating samples at relatively lower temperatures. Medium temperature muffle furnaces operate between 1,100°C and 1,400°C, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial and laboratory applications. High temperature muffle furnaces, on the other hand, can operate above 1,400°C and are commonly used in fields like materials science, ceramics, and metallurgy that require extremely high temperatures for heating and processing samples.</p></p>
