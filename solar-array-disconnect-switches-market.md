<p><p>The solar array disconnect switches market consists of two main types: AC disconnect switches and DC disconnect switches. DC disconnect switches perform a similar function but for the direct current (DC) power supply. Both types of switches play a critical role in enabling safe and efficient operation of solar arrays by providing an effective means to disconnect the power supply when needed.</p></p>
