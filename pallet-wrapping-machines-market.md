<p><p>The pallet wrapping machines market offers three types of machines - semi-automatic, fully automatic, and manual. Semi-automatic machines require some level of manual intervention, such as attaching the film to the pallet and starting the wrapping process. Fully automatic machines, on the other hand, are highly automated and require minimal manual effort as the entire wrapping operation is completed automatically. Manual machines, as the name suggests, involve entirely manual operations, including attaching the film and manually rotating the pallet to wrap it.</p></p>
