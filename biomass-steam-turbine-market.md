<p><p>The biomass steam turbine market can be classified into different types based on the operating conditions and design. Firstly, condensing turbines are designed to extract maximum energy from the biomass steam by condensing the exhaust steam into water. They are more efficient and are commonly used in large-scale power plants. Secondly, back pressure turbines generate both power and heat by extracting steam at a lower pressure. These are suitable for smaller-scale applications like district heating. Lastly, there are other types like extraction condensing turbines and extraction back pressure turbines, which offer specific advantages based on operational requirements.</p></p>
