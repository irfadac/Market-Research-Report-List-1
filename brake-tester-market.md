<p><p>Brake testers are market types used for testing the efficiency of vehicle brakes. Roller brake testers employ a set of rotating cylinders where the wheels of the vehicle rest on, measuring braking forces. Plate brake testers, on the other hand, use fixed plates where vehicle wheels are positioned for assessing brake performance. Decelerometers are portable devices that measure the rate of deceleration during a braking operation. Each of these market types offers different features and benefits for conducting brake tests, catering to varying needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
