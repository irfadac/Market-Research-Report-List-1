<p><p>The marine base steering gear market consists of two main types: ram type and rotary vane type. In the ram type, hydraulic cylinders or rams are used to generate the required force for steering the ship. On the other hand, the rotary vane type utilizes rotating vanes to produce the necessary power for steering. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper maneuvering and control of marine vessels, but they employ different mechanisms to achieve the desired outcome.</p></p>
