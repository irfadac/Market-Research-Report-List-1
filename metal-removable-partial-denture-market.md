<p><p>A metal removable partial denture (RPD) is a denture that replaces one or more missing teeth in the mouth. It consists of a metal framework with artificial teeth attached to it and is designed to be removable by the patient. Metal RPDs are commonly used when other tooth replacement options, such as dental implants or fixed bridges, are not suitable or desired by the patient.</p><p>The metal removable partial denture market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of dental disorders and tooth loss, rising geriatric population, and growing awareness about dental aesthetics. Additionally, advancements in dental technology and materials have made metal RPDs more durable, comfortable, and aesthetically pleasing, thereby boosting their adoption.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising demand for cosmetic dentistry and the increasing disposable income of individuals are also contributing to the market growth. Metal RPDs are an affordable tooth replacement option compared to dental implants or fixed bridges, making them accessible to a larger patient population.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the metal removable partial denture market is the use of CAD/CAM technology in the design and fabrication of these dentures. This technology allows for precise and accurate customization of metal RPDs based on the patient's oral anatomy, resulting in improved fit and patient satisfaction.</p><p>Overall, the metal removable partial denture market is expected to experience substantial growth during the forecast period, driven by factors such as increasing dental disorders, advancements in dental technology, and rising demand for cosmetic dentistry. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during this period.</p></p>
