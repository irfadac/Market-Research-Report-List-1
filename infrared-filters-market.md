<p><ul><li>Glass Type</li><li>Film Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared filter market is divided into two types: glass type and film type filters. Glass type filters are made of specific types of glass that can effectively block out visible light while allowing infrared light to pass through. These filters are used in applications such as infrared photography and thermal imaging. Film type filters, on the other hand, consist of a thin film coating that selectively absorbs visible light and transmits infrared light. These filters are commonly used in smartphone cameras and other compact devices for capturing infrared images.</p></p>
