<p><p>Sanitary ball valves are commonly used in industries where strict hygiene standards need to be maintained, such as food and beverage or pharmaceutical industries. Two common types of sanitary ball valves are clamp connections and weld connections. Clamp connections allow for easy installation and removal, making them suitable for applications that require frequent disassembly. Weld connections, on the other hand, provide a more permanent and secure connection, ideal for applications that require high pressure or temperature environments. Both types of connections are designed to ensure the safety and integrity of the system while meeting sanitary requirements.</p></p>
