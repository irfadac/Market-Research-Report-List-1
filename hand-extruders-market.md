<p><p>Hand extruders are widely used in various industries for small-scale extrusion processes. The market is categorized into two types: Twin Screw Hand Extruders and Single Screw Hand Extruders. Twin Screw Hand Extruders are equipped with two screws that rotate in opposite directions and offer enhanced mixing capabilities, making them suitable for processing a wide range of materials. On the other hand, Single Screw Hand Extruders have a single screw that moves material forward and are more commonly used for simpler extrusion applications. Both types cater to different processing needs in the extrusion industry.</p></p>
