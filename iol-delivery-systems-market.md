<p><ul><li>Preloaded IOL Delivery Systems</li><li>Non-Preloaded IOL Delivery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IOL Delivery Systems refer to devices used in cataract surgery to insert an intraocular lens (IOL) into the eye. Preloaded IOL Delivery Systems come with the IOL already loaded onto the delivery device, simplifying the surgical procedure as it eliminates the need for the surgeon to manually load the lens. Non-preloaded IOL Delivery Systems require the surgeon to manually load the IOL onto the device before insertion. Both systems aim to enhance efficiency and accuracy in cataract surgery, offering options for surgeons based on their preferences and surgical needs.</p></p>
