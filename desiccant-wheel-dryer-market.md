<p><p>A Desiccant Wheel Dryer is a type of industrial dryer commonly used to remove moisture from air or gas streams. It consists of a rotating wheel made of a desiccant material (such as silica gel or molecular sieves) that has a high affinity for attracting and adsorbing moisture. The wet air or gas passes through the wheel where the moisture is adsorbed, resulting in the drying of the air or gas stream.</p><p>The Desiccant Wheel Dryer Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
