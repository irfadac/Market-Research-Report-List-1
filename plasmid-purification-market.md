<p><p>The Plasmid Purification Market includes two major types: Plasmid DNA and Bacterial Artificial Chromosome (BAC) DNA. Plasmid DNA purification refers to the process of isolating and extracting plasmid DNA from bacterial cells. This DNA is often used in genetic engineering and biotechnology applications. On the other hand, BAC DNA purification involves the extraction of larger DNA fragments from bacterial cells using BAC vectors. BAC DNA is mainly used in genomic research, gene mapping, and cloning. Both types of purification offer valuable tools in various scientific and medical fields.</p></p>
