<p><p>ISO refrigerated containers, also known as reefer containers, are shipping containers that are specially designed to transport and store temperature-sensitive goods. They are equipped with a built-in refrigeration unit that helps to maintain a specific temperature range inside the container, regardless of the outside climate conditions.</p><p>These containers are widely used in the transportation of perishable goods such as fruits, vegetables, meat, dairy products, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. They offer a convenient and efficient solution for long-distance transportation, as they can maintain the desired temperature for an extended period, ensuring the quality and freshness of the cargo.</p><p>The ISO refrigerated container market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, and this trend is expected to continue during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing demand for frozen and chilled food products, growing international trade in perishable goods, and advancements in refrigeration technology. Additionally, the rising adoption of cold chain logistics in developing countries is also expected to drive the market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing preference for eco-friendly refrigerants and energy-efficient refrigeration systems in the ISO refrigerated container market. Companies are increasingly focusing on developing environmentally friendly solutions to reduce the carbon footprint associated with refrigeration. There is also a rising demand for smart reefer containers that can monitor and control temperature, humidity, and other parameters remotely.</p><p>Overall, the ISO refrigerated container market is expected to experience strong growth in the coming years, driven by the increasing demand for temperature-controlled transportation solutions and the introduction of innovative and sustainable technologies.
