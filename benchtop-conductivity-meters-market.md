<p><p>Benchtop conductivity meters are devices used to measure the electrical conductivity of a solution. The market for these meters can be classified into three types: single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel meters. Single channel meters can measure conductivity in only one sample at a time. Dual channel meters can measure conductivity in two samples simultaneously, facilitating comparison or analysis. Multi-channel meters have the capability to measure conductivity in multiple samples simultaneously, increasing efficiency and productivity in laboratories or research settings. This classification helps cater to various needs and requirements of users in terms of sample capacity and throughput.</p></p>
