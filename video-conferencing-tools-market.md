<p><p>The video conferencing tools market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. They offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of use, making them popular among businesses of all sizes. On the other hand, on-premises video conferencing tools require companies to install and manage the infrastructure on their own premises.
