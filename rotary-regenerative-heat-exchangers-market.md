<p><p>Rotary Regenerative Heat Exchangers can be classified into two main types based on their operations- Condensing Rotary Regenerative Heat Exchangers and Sorption Rotary Regenerative Heat Exchangers. Condensing Rotary Regenerative Heat Exchangers work by transferring heat from hot exhaust gases to incoming air, while Sorption Rotary Regenerative Heat Exchangers use a sorbent material to absorb and release water vapor to transfer heat. Both types of heat exchangers are used for energy recovery and increasing efficiency in various industrial processes.</p></p>
