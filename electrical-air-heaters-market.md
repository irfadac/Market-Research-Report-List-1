<p><p>Electrical air heaters are heating devices used in various industries to warm up air. Immersion heaters are inserted directly into the medium to provide heat, while tubular heaters consist of heating elements within a metal sheath. Circulation heaters circulate the medium through a heating element for efficient heat transfer. Band heaters are wrapped around pipes or cylinders, and strip heaters are flat heating elements. Coil heaters are designed as coiled wires, and other types include infrared heaters, forced air heaters, and radiant heaters. These heaters offer different solutions for diverse heating applications.</p></p>
