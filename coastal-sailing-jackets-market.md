<p><p>Coastal sailing jackets can be classified into two types: Gore-Tex and fleece. Gore-Tex jackets are specifically designed for outdoor activities and provide extreme waterproofing and breathability. They are made of a thin membrane that allows perspiration to escape while blocking water from entering. This ensures the wearer stays dry and comfortable in wet weather conditions. On the other hand, fleece jackets are made from synthetic materials that offer insulation and warmth. They are lightweight and quick drying, making them ideal for light rain or chilly coastal sailing excursions.</p></p>
