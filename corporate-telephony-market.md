<p><p>The Corporate Telephony Market consists of three main types: IP-PBX, Key Telephone System (KTS), and Wireless Private Branch Exchange (WPBX). IP-PBX uses internet protocol to connect and manage phone calls within an organization, providing advanced features and scalability. KTS allows users to directly select outside lines and access other functions, mainly used in smaller businesses. WPBX utilizes wireless technology to connect devices and extend telephony services to remote locations. These telephony systems cater to different organizational needs, offering various features and communication solutions.</p></p>
