<p><p>Cable ladder system market includes two major types: metal cable ladders and FPR (Fiber Reinforced Polymer) cable ladders. Metal cable ladders are made from steel or aluminum providing high strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty cable management applications. On the other hand, FPR cable ladders are constructed using fiberglass and resin materials, offering excellent corrosion resistance and lightweight characteristics, making them ideal for environments exposed to harsh chemicals or outdoor elements. Both types are widely used in various industries for organizing and supporting cables.</p></p>
