<p><p>The fixed tilt solar PV market consists of three main types: mono-Si, multi-Si, and thin film market. Mono-Si refers to monocrystalline silicon solar panels, which are made from a single crystal structure, providing higher efficiency and durability but at a higher cost. Multi-Si, on the other hand, uses polycrystalline silicon, which is less efficient but more affordable. Thin film market utilizes a different technology, wherein solar cells are made by depositing a thin layer of photovoltaic material onto a substrate. Thin film panels are cheaper and more flexible but have lower efficiency.</p></p>
