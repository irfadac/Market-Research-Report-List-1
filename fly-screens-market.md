<p><p>Fly screens, also known as insect screens or mosquito screens, are mesh barriers fitted to windows, doors, or other openings to prevent the entry of insects and flies while allowing fresh air to circulate. Fly screens are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial properties to maintain a hygienic environment and protect against vector-borne diseases caused by insects.</p><p>The current outlook of the fly screens market is promising, driven by increasing awareness about the importance of insect control, rising health concerns, and growing demand for energy-efficient and eco-friendly solutions. As the global population continues to grow, particularly in urban areas, the need for insect protection is becoming more critical.
