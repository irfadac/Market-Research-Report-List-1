<p><p>The seals and gaskets market is divided into two types: seals market and gaskets market. Seals are used to prevent the leaking of fluids or gases between two or more mechanical components. They are usually made of rubber or metal and come in various shapes and sizes. On the other hand, gaskets are flat components placed between two surfaces to create a static seal. They are commonly made of materials like rubber, silicone, or cork and are used to prevent leakage in systems with high pressures or extreme temperatures.</p></p>
