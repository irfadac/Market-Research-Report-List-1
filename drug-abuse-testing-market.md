<p><p>Drug abuse testing is a market segment that includes various types of testing. Random testing involves selecting individuals from a pool and testing them for drug abuse without any prior notice. Reasonable testing allows employers to test employees who show signs of drug abuse. Post-accident testing is conducted after any workplace accident occurs, aiming to determine if drug abuse was a contributing factor. Lastly, follow-up testing is performed regularly on individuals who have previously tested positive to ensure they remain drug-free. These types of testing are crucial for maintaining workplace safety and productivity.</p></p>
