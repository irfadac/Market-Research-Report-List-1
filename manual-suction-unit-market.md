<p><p>The manual suction unit market consists of two types: disposable and reusable units. Disposable suction units are designed for single-use and are discarded after each use, providing a hygienic and convenient solution. They are commonly used in hospitals and clinics to prevent cross-contamination. Reusable suction units, on the other hand, can be cleaned and sterilized for multiple uses, making them a more cost-effective option in the long run. They are commonly used in home healthcare settings or in situations where frequent suctioning is required.</p></p>
