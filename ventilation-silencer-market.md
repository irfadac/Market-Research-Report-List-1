<p><p>The ventilation silencer market encompasses various types of silencers used in ventilation systems. Intake and discharge silencers are designed to reduce noise generated by the intake and discharge of air, ensuring a quieter environment. Duct silencers are installed within the air ducts to attenuate noise transmission throughout the system. Additionally, there are other types of ventilation silencers available, including inline silencers, terminal unit silencers, and plenum silencers, each serving specific purposes to control noise levels in ventilation systems. These silencers are essential in minimizing noise pollution and creating a more pleasant indoor environment.</p></p>
