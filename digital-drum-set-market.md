<p><strong>The Digital Drum Set Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rubber Heads</li><li>Mylar Heads</li><li>Mesh or Silicone Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital drum set market offers different types of drum heads, including rubber heads, mylar heads, and mesh or silicone heads. Rubber heads are made of a thick layer of rubber, providing enhanced durability and a bouncier feel. Mylar heads, on the other hand, are constructed of a thin plastic film over a metal ring, producing a crisper sound and better sensitivity. Lastly, mesh or silicone heads use a mesh or silicone material, offering a quieter playing experience and natural rebound response. Each type caters to different player preferences and desired sound characteristics.</p></p>
