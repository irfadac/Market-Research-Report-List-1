<p><strong>The Medical Micro Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Magnetic Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical micro pumps are devices used in the healthcare industry to administer medication in a controlled manner. Electric micro pumps are powered by electricity and use electromagnetic forces to generate pressure for pumping fluids. They provide accurate dosing and are commonly used in insulin delivery systems. On the other hand, magnetic drive micro pumps operate through a magnetic coupling, which eliminates the need for a shaft seal. This makes them suitable for applications requiring leak-free pumping, such as in dialysis machines. Both types enhance medication delivery precision, ensuring effective treatment.</p></p>
