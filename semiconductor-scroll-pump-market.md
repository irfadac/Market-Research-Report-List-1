<p><ul><li>Static Vortex</li><li>Moving Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor scroll pump market can be divided into two types: static vortex and moving vortex. Static vortex refers to a type of pump that creates a swirling motion in a stationary manner. It utilizes static components to generate the vortex effect, enabling efficient pumping of semiconductors. On the other hand, moving vortex pumps use moving components to generate the swirling motion. They offer enhanced performance and higher pumping speeds compared to static vortex pumps. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by efficiently pumping gases during manufacturing processes.</p></p>
