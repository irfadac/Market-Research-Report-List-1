<p><strong>The VOC Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC Analyzer market is comprised of two major types: PID (Photo Ionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor. PID analyzers use ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and measure their concentration levels. These analyzers are highly sensitive and can detect a wide range of VOCs in various applications such as environmental monitoring and safety. On the other hand, Metal-oxide Semiconductor analyzers rely on a semiconductor sensor to detect VOCs. They are cost-effective and commonly used in indoor air quality monitoring, industrial hygiene, and automotive emission testing.</p></p>
