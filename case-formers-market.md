<p><p>Case formers are machines used in packaging industries to form cases or boxes for products. There are two main market types of case formers: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. </p><p>Semi-automatic case formers require manual intervention to load a flat box, which is then formed and taped by the machine. They are suitable for businesses with lower production volumes and limited space.</p><p>Fully-automatic case formers, on the other hand, can handle high production volumes without human assistance. They automatically form, fold, and tape boxes, maximizing efficiency and minimizing labor costs. These are ideal for larger businesses with high production demands.</p></p>
