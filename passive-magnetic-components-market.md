<p><p>Passive magnetic components are vital electronic components that do not require an external power source to function. Transformers are devices that transfer electrical energy between two or more circuits through electromagnetic induction. They are commonly used to step up or step down voltage levels. On the other hand, inductors are components that store energy in a magnetic field when current flows through them. They are primarily used to control current flow, filter out undesirable frequencies, and store energy in electronic devices. Both transformers and inductors play crucial roles in various applications across industries.</p></p>
