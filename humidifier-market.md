<p><p>The humidifier market can be categorized into different types based on their functioning mechanism. Ultrasonic humidifiers use ultrasonic vibrations to generate a cool mist, making them energy-efficient and quiet. Evaporative humidifiers work by blowing moisture through a wet wick or filter, which evaporates and increases humidity levels. Other types of humidifiers include steam humidifiers, impeller humidifiers, and central humidifiers. Each type has unique advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs and preferences.</p></p>
