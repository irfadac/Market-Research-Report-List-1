<p><p>In the food and beverage industry, industrial automation refers to the use of technology and control systems to streamline and optimize production processes. PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) is a digital computer used for automation and control of industrial processes. SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) system is used to monitor, gather, and analyze real-time data. MES (Manufacturing Execution System) provides real-time tracking and monitoring of production processes, inventory, and quality control.</p></p>
