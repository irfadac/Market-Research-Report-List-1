<p><strong>The Dental Digital X-ray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraoral X-ray System</li><li>Intraoral X-ray System</li><li>Hybrid X-ray Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental digital X-ray market includes three types of systems: Extraoral X-ray System, Intraoral X-ray System, and Hybrid X-ray Systems. Extraoral X-ray systems capture images outside the mouth, such as panoramic or cephalometric X-rays. Intraoral X-ray systems are placed inside the mouth to capture detailed images of individual teeth and their surrounding structures. Hybrid X-ray systems combine features of both extraoral and intraoral systems to provide a comprehensive view of the oral cavity. Each type offers unique benefits for dental professionals in diagnosing and treating oral health issues.</p></p>
