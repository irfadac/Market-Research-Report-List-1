<p><p>Gearless traction elevators are a type of elevator system that utilize a gearless motor to move the elevator car along vertical shafts. Unlike geared traction elevators, which require a gearbox to transfer power from the motor to the sheave, gearless traction elevators have a direct connection to the sheave, eliminating the need for gears. This design offers several advantages including energy efficiency, smoother operation, higher speed, and lower maintenance requirements.</p><p>The gearless traction elevators market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Rapid urbanization and the increasing construction of high-rise buildings are the key factors driving the market growth. As cities become more crowded and land becomes scarce, buildings are being constructed taller and more vertical transportation systems are needed to transport people efficiently.</p><p>Furthermore, the emphasis on energy efficiency and sustainability in building designs has also contributed to the demand for gearless traction elevators. These elevators use less energy compared to traditional geared traction elevators, making them more environmentally friendly.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have led to the development of gearless traction elevators with improved safety features, such as advanced control systems and emergency evacuation systems. These factors are expected to drive the adoption of gearless traction elevators in various end-user industries, including residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p><p>In conclusion, the gearless traction elevators market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period, driven by factors such as urbanization, increasing construction of high-rise buildings, and the emphasis on energy efficiency. The market is also witnessing advancements in technology, leading to the development of safer and more efficient elevators.</p></p>
