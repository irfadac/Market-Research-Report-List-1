<p><p>The helicopter MRO (Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul) market consists of three main types: airframe heavy maintenance, engine maintenance, and component maintenance. Airframe heavy maintenance involves extensive inspection and repair of the helicopter's structure and major systems. Engine maintenance focuses on the proper functioning of the aircraft's engines, including regular servicing and repairs as needed. Component maintenance deals with the inspection, refurbishment, and replacement of individual helicopter components such as avionics, landing gear, and rotor blades to ensure they meet safety standards and extend their lifespan. These segments collectively address the comprehensive maintenance needs of helicopter operators.</p></p>
