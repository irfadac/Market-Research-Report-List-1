<p><p>The VR handle market consists of two major types: signal controllers and game controllers. Signal controllers are designed to translate the user's hand and finger movements into virtual reality. They can detect a wide range of motions and accurately replicate them in the virtual world. On the other hand, game controllers are specially designed for gaming experiences in virtual reality. These controllers typically resemble traditional gaming controllers and offer enhanced features such as haptic feedback and motion tracking for a more immersive gaming experience.
