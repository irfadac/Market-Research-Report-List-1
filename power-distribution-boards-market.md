<p><p>Power Distribution Boards are essential components in electrical systems that help distribute power from the main source to various circuits or appliances. A.C Power Distribution Boards are specifically designed to handle alternating current and are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. These boards ensure safe and efficient power distribution by regulating voltage levels and providing circuit protection. On the other hand, D.C Power Distribution Boards are designed for direct current applications and are often utilized in telecommunications, data centers, and automotive industries. They perform similar functions as A.C boards but are tailored for D.C power supply requirements.</p></p>
