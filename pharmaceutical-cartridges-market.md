<p><ul><li>Glass</li><li>Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical cartridges market is divided into two main types based on the materials used: glass and plastic. Glass cartridges are made from high-quality borosilicate glass and are known for their excellent chemical resistance and low extractables. They are often preferred for drug formulations that are sensitive to interaction with plastic materials. On the other hand, plastic cartridges are made from polymeric materials such as cyclic olefin copolymer (COC) or polyethylene terephthalate (PET). They offer advantages such as being lightweight, break-resistant, and cost-effective. Both types cater to the packaging needs of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
