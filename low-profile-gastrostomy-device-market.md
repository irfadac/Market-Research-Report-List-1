<p><p>The Low Profile Gastrostomy Device market comprises two types: Obturated and Non-obturated. In the Obturated market, the device has a silicone balloon that is inflated inside the stomach to maintain its position. This type is preferred for long-term use. On the other hand, the Non-obturated market offers a lower profile device without a balloon, which is suitable for short-term requirements. Both devices provide a feeding tube inserted directly into the stomach, but their design and features differ based on the patient's needs and duration of usage.</p></p>
