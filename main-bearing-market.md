<p><strong>The Main Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sliding Bearing</li><li>Rolling Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The main bearing market can be divided into two types: sliding bearing and rolling bearing. Sliding bearings have a smooth surface and rely on lubrication to minimize friction between the moving parts. They are commonly used in heavy-duty applications and provide high load-bearing capabilities. On the other hand, rolling bearings feature rolling elements such as balls or rollers to reduce friction. They offer lower friction, higher precision, and longer service life, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in supporting rotating shafts, optimizing performance, and ensuring smooth operations.</p></p>
