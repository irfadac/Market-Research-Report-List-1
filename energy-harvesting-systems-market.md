The market types in this sector include photovoltaic, thermoelectric, piezo, and electrodynamic systems. Photovoltaic systems utilize solar energy to generate electricity, while thermoelectric systems convert waste heat into power. Piezo systems convert mechanical stress or vibrations into electrical energy, and electrodynamic systems harvest energy through electromagnetic induction. These systems are designed to capture and convert ambient energy to meet the growing demand for sustainable and self-powered electronic devices and applications.</p></p>
