Consumers following plant-based diets seek products that align with their lifestyle choices, including products colored with natural ingredients.</p><p>The latest trend in the natural colorants market is the development of innovative and sustainable extraction techniques. <p><p>The natural colorants market can be divided into three types based on their source: those obtained from plants, animals, and minerals. Colorants obtained from plants refer to pigments derived from various parts of plants, such as flowers, fruits, leaves, or roots. Animal-based colorants are obtained from sources like insects or sea organisms. Mineral-based colorants are derived from minerals like clay, iron oxide, or titanium dioxide. These natural colorants are used in a wide range of industries, including food and beverages, cosmetics, textiles, and pharmaceuticals, to achieve desired colors naturally.</p></p>
