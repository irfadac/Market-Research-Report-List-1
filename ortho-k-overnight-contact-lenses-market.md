<p><p>Orthokeratology (Ortho-k) is a non-surgical process that uses specially designed contact lenses to reshape the cornea overnight. Ortho-k offers an alternative to traditional eyeglasses, daytime contact lenses, or refractive surgery.</p><p>The Ortho-k Overnight Contact Lenses Market is witnessing significant growth, mainly driven by increasing awareness about myopia management and the desire for a non-surgical solution. Manufacturers are focusing on developing lenses with higher oxygen permeability to ensure corneal health during overnight wear. Moreover, innovative technologies like wavefront-guided orthokeratology are being introduced to further enhance the accuracy and customization of lens fittings.</p><p>The market is witnessing increasing adoption of Ortho-k by athletes and individuals engaged in sports activities.
