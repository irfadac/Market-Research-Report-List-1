<p><p>The hydroxylated epoxidized soybean oil market includes two different types of modes of production: ring opening polymerization and reaction with maleic anhydride. In the ring opening polymerization process, the epoxidized soybean oil undergoes a reaction where the epoxy rings are opened and polymerized to form a hydroxylated product. On the other hand, in the reaction with maleic anhydride process, the epoxidized soybean oil reacts with maleic anhydride to produce a hydroxylated product. These different modes of production result in variations in the properties and applications of the hydroxylated epoxidized soybean oil.</p></p>
