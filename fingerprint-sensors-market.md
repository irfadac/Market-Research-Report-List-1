<p><strong>The Fingerprint Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Fingerprint sensors are widely used for identification and security purposes. Capacitive sensors, the most common type, measure the electrical current produced when a fingerprint touches the sensor. Optical sensors capture an image of the fingerprint using light-sensitive lenses. Thermal sensors detect the temperature difference between ridges and valleys on a fingerprint. Other types may include ultrasonic sensors that use high-frequency sound waves or pressure-sensitive sensors. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, and they are utilized in various applications such as smartphones, access control systems, and payment devices.</p></p>
