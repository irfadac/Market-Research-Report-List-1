<p><strong>The Wireless Charging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Charging Receiver</li><li>Wireless Charging Transmitter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless Charging Receiver market refers to the segment that provides devices with the capability to receive power wirelessly. These receivers are integrated into various devices such as smartphones, tablets, and wearables, enabling them to charge without the need for physical connections. On the other hand, Wireless Charging Transmitter market includes devices that transmit power wirelessly to the receivers. These transmitters are standalone devices or integrated into charging pads or stations, and they deliver power to compatible receivers within their range, eliminating the need for cables or plugs.</p></p>
