<p><p>Electrically Active Smart Glass and Windows refer to glass materials that can alter their transparency or reflectivity with the application of an electric current. There are various types of Electrically Active Smart Glass including Electrochromic, which changes opacity through oxidation and reduction reactions; Thermochromic, which alters transparency based on temperature changes; SPD Smart Glass, which uses suspended particle devices to control light transmission; and PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal), which uses polymer films with dispersed liquid crystals to modify the glass's transparency. These technologies offer customizable control over light and privacy in windows and glass surfaces.</p></p>
