<p><p>Live streaming pay-per-view (PPV) market can be classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. </p><p>1. Cloud Market: In this type, the live streaming PPV service is hosted on remote servers managed by a third-party provider. The cloud market offers scalability, flexibility, and ease of use as the infrastructure and maintenance responsibilities are handled by the service provider. Users can access the live PPV content from anywhere with internet connectivity.</p><p>2. On-Premises Market: In contrast, the on-premises market involves hosting the live streaming PPV service on local servers within an organization's infrastructure. This type gives users complete control over data privacy and security. However, it requires dedicated resources for setup, management, and maintenance, leading to higher costs and limited scalability compared to the cloud market.</p></p>
