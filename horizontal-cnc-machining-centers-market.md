<p><p>Horizontal CNC machining centers are versatile machines used in various industries for precision machining tasks. These centers are categorized based on the number of axes they operate on. </p><p>3-axis machines move along the X, Y, and Z axes, allowing for basic operations. 4-axis machines have an additional rotary axis, enabling more complex operations like indexing or continuous 4th-axis milling. 5-axis machines possess an additional tilting rotary axis, enhancing flexibility and precision in machining complex shapes. 7-axis machines have two additional horizontal axes, providing even greater maneuverability in machining operations. Other types may include machines with specialized axes or features beyond the standard configurations.</p></p>
