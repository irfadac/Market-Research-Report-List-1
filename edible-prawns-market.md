<p><p>The edible prawns market can be categorized into two types: seawater shrimp and freshwater shrimp. Seawater shrimp are prawns that are typically harvested from saltwater environments, such as oceans or seas. They have a distinct briny flavor and are highly sought after for their firm texture and versatility in cooking. On the other hand, freshwater shrimp are prawns that are found in freshwater bodies like lakes or rivers. They tend to have a milder taste and slightly softer texture compared to seawater shrimp. Both types cater to different culinary preferences and have their own unique market demand.</p></p>
