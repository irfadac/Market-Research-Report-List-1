<p><p>Air Start Units (ASUs) are essential ground support equipment used to start aircraft engines. ASUs are available in two market types: Diesel and Gasoline. Diesel ASUs run on diesel fuel, which is readily available and often more cost-effective. They are commonly used in military and commercial aviation, providing a reliable and efficient source of compressed air for engine starting. Gasoline ASUs, on the other hand, utilize gasoline as their fuel source. They are typically used in smaller aviation operations or in situations where diesel fuel may not be as easily accessible. Both types of ASUs contribute to the smooth and safe start-up of aircraft engines.</p></p>
