<p><p>Antimicrobial wound dressings are products used to prevent or treat wound infections. Silver wound dressings contain silver that releases antimicrobial ions, killing bacteria on the wound. Iodine antimicrobial dressings release iodine, which has broad-spectrum antimicrobial properties. Honey antimicrobial dressings use medical-grade honey to inhibit the growth of bacteria and promote wound healing. "Others" includes various types of antimicrobial dressings, such as those containing polyhexamethylene biguanide (PHMB) or chlorhexidine. These dressings aim to provide an antimicrobial barrier, prevent infection, and accelerate wound healing.</p></p>
