<p><p>The cell culture tools market comprises various types of equipment used for growing and studying cells in laboratories. Chamber slides are glass or plastic slides with wells for holding cells. Plates are multi-well plates made of plastic or glass and come in different sizes with wells for cell culture. Flasks are vessels with a neck and a cap, used for growing cells in large volumes. Dishes are sterile plastic or glass containers with a flat bottom, designed for cell culture. Filtration market includes filters used for separating cells or particles from liquid culture media.</p></p>
