<p><p>The Hemorrhoid Treatment Devices Market includes several types of devices used to treat hemorrhoids. Band ligators are used to place rubber bands around the base of hemorrhoids, cutting off their blood supply and causing them to shrink. Sclerotherapy injectors are utilized to inject a chemical solution into hemorrhoids, causing them to shrink. Infrared coagulators use heat to coagulate the blood vessels supplying the hemorrhoids. Hemorrhoid laser probes use laser energy to shrink and remove hemorrhoids. Other devices in the market include staplers, cryotherapy devices, and electric coagulation devices.</p></p>
