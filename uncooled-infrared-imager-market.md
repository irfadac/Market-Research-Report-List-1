<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Imager Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared Sensors</li><li>Active Infrared Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled infrared imager market encompasses two main types: passive infrared sensors and active infrared sensors. Passive infrared sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects and convert it into a visual image, ideal for applications like night vision and thermal imaging. Active infrared sensors, on the other hand, emit their own infrared radiation and measure its reflection to identify objects or detect motion, commonly used in security systems and occupancy detection. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, such as surveillance, automotive, and medical imaging.</p></p>
