Many self-service ordering machines now provide options for customers to pay using mobile wallets or scanning QR codes, eliminating the need for physical cash or cards. This trend is particularly popular in regions with high smartphone penetration rates, as it simplifies the payment process for customers.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the customization and personalization of orders.
