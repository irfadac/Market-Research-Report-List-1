<p><p>The sterile surgical sutures market consists of two main types of products: sterile absorbable surgical yarns and sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns. Sterile absorbable surgical yarns are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. On the other hand, sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns are made from materials that do not break down and are not absorbed by the body, requiring removal once the wound has healed. These different types of sutures provide healthcare professionals with options to meet specific patient needs and wound characteristics.</p></p>
