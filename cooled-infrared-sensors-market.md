<p><p>Cooled infrared sensors can be categorized into two main types: thermal and quantum. Thermal infrared sensors detect heat emitted by objects and convert it into an image, making them ideal for applications such as night vision and surveillance. Quantum infrared sensors, on the other hand, operate by detecting individual photons and are highly sensitive, making them suitable for scientific research and medical imaging. Both types of sensors offer unique advantages and are used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
