<p><p>The air flow sensor, used in automobiles, can be categorized into two types: Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor and Volumetric Air Flow Sensor. Mass Air Flow Sensor measures the amount of air entering the engine, based on the temperature, pressure, and density, to ensure optimal fuel injection. On the other hand, Volumetric Air Flow Sensor calculates the volumetric flow of air by examining the speed of the incoming air. These sensors play a crucial role in maintaining proper engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
