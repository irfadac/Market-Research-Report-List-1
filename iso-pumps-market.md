<p><p>ISO pumps refer to a classification system for pumps set by the International Organization for Standardization. There are three market types within the ISO pumps classification. Centrifugal pumps are commonly used in industries for their ability to efficiently transport liquids through the use of centrifugal force. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, deliver fluid by trapping liquid between moving parts. Lastly, the "other" market includes pumps that do not fit into the centrifugal or positive displacement category, such as diaphragm pumps or specialty pumps tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
