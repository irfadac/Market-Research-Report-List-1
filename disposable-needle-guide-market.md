Moreover, the rising geriatric population, who are more prone to such diseases, further contributes to the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, there is a growing demand for minimally invasive procedures due to their benefits such as reduced hospital stays, faster recovery, and minimal scarring. Disposable needle guides play a crucial role in performing these procedures with precision and accuracy.</p><p>The market is also witnessing various trends that are driving its growth. One such trend is the increasing adoption of ultrasound-guided procedures. Ultrasound-guided needle procedures are being preferred over traditional methods due to their real-time imaging capabilities, higher accuracy, and reduced risks.
