<p><p>Roll-fed thermoforming machines are used in the manufacturing process of plastic products by heating a roll of plastic sheet and forming it into different shapes. The market is categorized into two types: automatic machines and fully-automatic machines. Automatic machines require manual intervention for certain tasks, such as loading the roll of plastic sheet, while the rest of the process is automated. Fully-automatic machines, on the other hand, are completely automated, requiring minimal human intervention. These machines offer higher efficiency, productivity, and reduced labor costs compared to automatic machines.</p></p>
