<p><strong>The Power Over Ethernet Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controller</li><li>PoE Powered Devices (PD) Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) Controller market consists of two main types: PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controller and PoE Powered Devices (PD) Controller. PSE Controllers are devices that provide power to PDs, such as IP cameras or wireless access points, through an Ethernet connection. They manage the power distribution and control the amount of power delivered to each PD. On the other hand, PD Controllers are embedded in devices that receive power through PoE, allowing them to efficiently manage and utilize the power received for their specific functionalities.</p></p>
