<p><p>TC Bonder, also known as thermosetting compound bonder, is a type of adhesive used in various industries for bonding applications. TC bonders provide strong and durable bonds while reducing the overall weight of the final product.</p><p>Additionally, the growing electronics industry is fueling the demand for TC bonders as they offer excellent electrical insulation properties. With the increasing use of electronic devices and components in various sectors, including smartphones, tablets, and automotive electronics, the demand for TC bonders is expected to rise significantly.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative TC bonders with improved properties such as thermal conductivity, chemical resistance, and flame retardancy.
