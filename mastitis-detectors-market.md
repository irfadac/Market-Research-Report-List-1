<p><p>The Mastitis Detectors market is categorized into two types: Mounted Detectors and Portable Detectors. Mounted Detectors are fixed devices that are attached to the cow's udder in a dairy farm. They provide continuous monitoring of the cow's milk quality and detect any signs of mastitis. On the other hand, Portable Detectors are handheld devices that allow farmers to easily check for mastitis in individual cows during milking. They offer flexibility and convenience in identifying infected cows, ensuring prompt treatment and preventing the spread of mastitis.</p></p>
