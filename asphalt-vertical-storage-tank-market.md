<p><p>The Asphalt Vertical Storage Tank market can be classified into three types based on heating methods: Conduction Oil Heating, Electric Heating, and Direct Heating. Conduction Oil Heating involves using a thermal fluid called conduction oil to heat the tank. Electric Heating involves the use of electric heaters placed inside the tank to generate heat. Direct Heating uses burners or heaters directly attached to the tank for heating purposes. These heating methods play a crucial role in maintaining the asphalt's desired temperature and viscosity for storage and transportation.</p></p>
