<p><p>The medical image analysis system market includes two main types: online systems and offline systems. Online systems refer to image analysis that is performed in real-time or near real-time, typically using cloud-based technology. These systems allow for instant access to medical images and can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, offline systems involve analysis that is conducted offline, meaning the images are stored and processed locally on a computer or server. These systems are typically used when internet connectivity is limited, and may provide greater security and privacy for sensitive medical data.</p></p>
