<p><ul><li>Traditional Services Type</li><li>Memorial Services Type</li><li>Immediate Service Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Funeral services market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. Traditional Services Type: This type involves a formal funeral service where the deceased's body is present for viewing or visitation. It usually follows a set of established rituals and customs, including prayers, readings, eulogies, and a procession to the burial site.</p><p>2. Memorial Services Type: Unlike traditional services, memorial services focus on celebrating the life of the deceased rather than displaying the body. These services can be held at any location and at any time after the person's passing. They may include personal tributes, music, photo displays, and storytelling.</p><p>3. Immediate Service Type: This type usually takes place soon after death, providing a simple and prompt service without embalming or public visitation. Immediate services are often chosen when the family wishes for a quick disposition, such as direct cremation or immediate burial, without extensive formalities.</p></p>
