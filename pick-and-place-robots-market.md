<p><p>The pick and place robots market is categorized into different types based on their design and structure. Articulated robots are versatile and have multiple joints, allowing them to move like a human arm. Delta robots are similar to articulated robots but use a different mechanism that offers high-speed and precise movements. SCARA robots are compact and excel in assembly line processes. Cartesian robots have a linear movement and are suitable for simple pick and place tasks. These different market types cater to specific industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
