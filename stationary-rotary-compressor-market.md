<p><p>The stationary rotary compressor market consists of two main types: AC and DC. AC refers to alternating current, which is the type of electrical current commonly used in households and commercial buildings. AC rotary compressors are designed to run on AC power and are widely used in various applications, such as air conditioning systems and industrial processes. On the other hand, DC stands for direct current, which is commonly used in batteries and automotive applications. DC rotary compressors are specifically designed to operate on DC power sources, such as batteries or solar panels, making them suitable for portable or off-grid applications.</p></p>
