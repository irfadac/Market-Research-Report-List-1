<p><p>Hydrophilic foam wound dressing market includes two types: without adhesive border and with adhesive border. On the other hand, with adhesive border dressings have an additional adhesive border that secures the dressing in place and prevents leakage. These dressings are suitable for wounds with moderate to heavy exudate, ensuring a secure and hygienic healing environment while promoting moisture retention and facilitating wound healing.</p></p>
