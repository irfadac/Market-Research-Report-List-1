<p><strong>The Silage Inoculants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Inoculants</li><li>Dry Inoculants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silage inoculants are additives used to improve the fermentation process and enhance the quality of silage, which is fermented forage used as animal feed. Wet inoculants, also known as liquid inoculants, are applied directly to the forage before it is ensiled. They contain live bacteria that help promote fermentation. Dry inoculants, on the other hand, are added in powder or granular form either directly to the forage or mixed with water before application. They typically contain dried or lyophilized bacteria that activate upon rehydration. Both wet and dry inoculants aim to improve silage fermentation and nutritional value.</p></p>
