<p><p>Dynamic water cutting machines are utilized in various industries for precise and efficient cutting processes. Sandless cutting is a type of dynamic water cutting that employs a high-pressure water jet without the use of abrasive particles. This method is suitable for delicate materials or when avoiding surface damage is essential. Conversely, abrasive cutting involves the addition of abrasive particles to the water jet, increasing its cutting power and making it suitable for thicker and harder materials. Both sandless cutting and abrasive cutting methods cater to specific cutting requirements and offer different advantages depending on the material being cut.</p></p>
