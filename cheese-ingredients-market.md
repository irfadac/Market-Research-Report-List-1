<p><p>The cheese ingredients market comprises two main types: natural and processed cheese. Natural cheese includes varieties like Cheddar, Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Gouda, which are made from milk and/or whey. These cheeses undergo a traditional aging process and have distinct flavors and textures. On the other hand, processed cheese is made by melting natural cheese and adding emulsifiers, stabilizers, and flavor enhancers. Processed cheese is known for its smooth and consistent texture and is often used in processed food products such as sandwiches, cheese spreads, and prepared meals.</p></p>
