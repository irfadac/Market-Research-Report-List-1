<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Doppler Diagnostic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasound Doppler Vascular Imaging</li><li>Color Doppler Flow Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic Doppler diagnostic equipment consists of two main types: ultrasound Doppler vascular imaging and color Doppler flow imaging. Ultrasound Doppler vascular imaging involves using ultrasonic waves to evaluate blood flow and detect any abnormalities in blood vessels, particularly in the arms and legs. Color Doppler flow imaging, on the other hand, combines traditional ultrasound imaging with color mapping to provide a visual representation of blood flow within the body, helping medical professionals analyze blood circulation and diagnose various vascular conditions.</p></p>
