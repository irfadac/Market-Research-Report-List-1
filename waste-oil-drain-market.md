<p><ul><li>Conventional</li><li>Self-extraction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waste oil drain market can be categorized into two types: conventional and self-extraction. Conventional waste oil drains typically require manual effort to extract the oil, using methods like tilting or gravity drainage. On the other hand, self-extraction waste oil drains have mechanisms that allow for easier and more efficient extraction, often utilizing pumps or suction systems. Both types serve the purpose of removing waste oil from vehicles or machinery, but self-extraction drains provide a more convenient and automated solution.</p></p>
