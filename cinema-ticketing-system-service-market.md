<p><p>Cinema ticketing system services are offered in different market types, including on-premise and cloud-based markets. On the other hand, the cloud-based market provides ticketing services through internet-based platforms. In this market, the ticketing system is hosted and managed by a third-party provider, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced maintenance for cinemas.
