<p><p>The Ultrasonic Proximity Sensors market is categorized into two types: Analog Type and Digital Type. Analog Type sensors detect the presence of an object by converting sound waves into electrical signals with varying voltage levels, providing continuous and proportional output. On the other hand, Digital Type sensors convert sound waves into digital signals, providing discrete output such as presence/absence of an object. Both types have their specific applications depending on the required functionality and precision.</p></p>
