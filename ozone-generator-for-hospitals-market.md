<p><p>The ozone generator for hospitals market offers three types of technologies. The high voltage discharge type utilizes an electric current to generate ozone through a corona discharge process. UV irradiation type uses ultraviolet light to convert oxygen into ozone. The electrolysis type involves passing an electric current through water containing dissolved oxygen to produce ozone. All three types are designed to generate ozone for use in hospitals, helping to eliminate odors, disinfect air and surfaces, and control the spread of bacteria and other pathogens.</p></p>
