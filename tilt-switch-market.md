<p><p>The tilt switch market can be classified into two types: mercury-free tilt switches and mercury tilt switches. Mercury-free tilt switches are a newer and more environmentally friendly alternative to traditional mercury switches. They use alternative materials, such as gases or liquids, to detect changes in orientation and activate a circuit. On the other hand, mercury tilt switches rely on the use of mercury to enable the switching action. However, due to the harmful effects of mercury on the environment and human health, their usage is becoming less common.</p></p>
