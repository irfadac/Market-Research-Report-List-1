<p><p>The aluminum diaphragm pump market consists of two main types: single acting and double acting. In single acting pumps, the diaphragm moves in one direction during each stroke, pushing the fluid out of the pump. These pumps are commonly used in applications where a steady and continuous flow is required. On the other hand, double acting pumps have a reciprocating diaphragm that moves in both directions during each stroke, allowing for a more efficient and higher flow rate. These pumps are often used in applications that require higher pressures or variable flow rates.</p></p>
