Collagen-based sealants contain collagen proteins that help in wound closure and tissue regeneration. Other types of biological sealants include synthetic sealants and hydrogels, which have diverse applications in surgical procedures. These sealants offer effective alternatives to sutures and staples, providing better patient outcomes, reduced healing time, and minimal scarring.</p></p>
