<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Hair Dryer</li><li>Axial Hair Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wall-mounted hair dryer market consists of two main types: centrifugal hair dryer and axial hair dryer. Centrifugal hair dryers use centrifugal force to push air out of the dryer, resulting in a more powerful airflow. They are generally more efficient and provide faster drying times. On the other hand, axial hair dryers use axial fans to generate airflow, which tends to be less powerful but quieter. Both types are popular choices for wall-mounted hair dryers, offering different advantages depending on the user's preferences and requirements.</p></p>
