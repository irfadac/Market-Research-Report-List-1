<p><strong>The Carbon Management Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Software - Counts Only Direct CO2 from Fuel</li><li>Software - Count Direct CO2</li><li>Software - Count Indirect CO2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon management software is classified into three types: </p><p>1. Software that counts only direct CO2 from fuel: This type of software focuses solely on quantifying the emissions produced from burning fossil fuels. It tracks and analyzes the direct carbon dioxide emissions generated by an organization's fuel consumption.</p><p>2. Software that counts direct CO2: This software encompasses a broader approach by not only considering emissions from fuel but also other direct sources, such as industrial processes or on-site energy production.</p><p>3. Software that counts indirect CO2: This type of software goes even further by including indirect emissions, such as those generated through electricity consumption or the supply chain. It provides a comprehensive assessment of all carbon dioxide emissions associated with an organization's activities.</p></p>
