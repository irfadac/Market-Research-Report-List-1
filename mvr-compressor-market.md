<p><strong>The MVR Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Type</li><li>Roots Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MVR (Mechanical Vapor Recompression) compressor market includes various types, two of which are the centrifugal type and the Roots type. Centrifugal compressors use high-speed rotating impellers to produce compression, offering high efficiency and low maintenance requirements, making them ideal for large-capacity applications. On the other hand, Roots compressors operate by utilizing two meshing rotating lobes, resulting in a pulsation-free and continuous flow of vapor. Although they are less efficient than centrifugal compressors, Roots compressors are often preferred for low-pressure ratio applications and when handling high molecular weight gases.</p></p>
