<p><p>Gel electrophoresis devices are essential tools in molecular biology research to separate and analyze DNA, RNA, and proteins. Horizontal gel electrophoresis devices feature a gel platform where samples are loaded and run horizontally, making them ideal for nucleic acid separation. Vertical gel electrophoresis devices consist of two glass plates with a gel enclosed between them, suitable for protein separation. Both these devices are widely used in various fields, including genetic testing, forensics, and biomedical research, to study and understand molecular components and structures.</p></p>
