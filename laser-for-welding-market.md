Automation in laser welding enables faster and more accurate welding processes, reducing the need for human intervention and increasing productivity. Additionally, the use of robotics allows for precise control and flexibility in handling complex weld geometries.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing adoption of fiber lasers for welding applications.
