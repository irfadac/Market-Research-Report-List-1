<p><p>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) Injection Molding Machinery is a type of equipment used in the manufacturing process of liquid silicone rubber products. LSR is a high-quality material known for its excellent biocompatibility, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making it suitable for a wide range of applications in industries such as healthcare, automotive, and consumer goods.</p><p>LSR injection molding machinery is used to inject liquid silicone rubber into a mold cavity, where it is cooled and solidifies to form the desired product. The machinery consists of an injection unit, a mold clamping unit, and a control system. It offers various advantages such as high precision, faster cycle times, low material wastage, and the ability to produce complex geometries.</p><p>The market for LSR injection molding machinery is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
