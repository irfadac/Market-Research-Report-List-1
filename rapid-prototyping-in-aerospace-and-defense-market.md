<p><strong>The Rapid Prototyping in Aerospace and Defense Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereolithogrphy Apparatus (SLA)</li><li>Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Three Dimension Printing (3DP)</li><li>Fused Depostion Modeling (FDM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid prototyping is widely used in the aerospace and defense industry. Stereolithography Apparatus (SLA) uses a laser to cure liquid resin, creating a solid, accurate prototype. Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) builds models by layering and cutting sheets of material. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) fuses powdered material together using a laser, allowing for intricate designs. Three Dimension Printing (3DP) uses a binder to create prototypes layer by layer. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes melted plastic filament to build models. These techniques enable quick and cost-effective production of prototypes for testing and validation.</p></p>
