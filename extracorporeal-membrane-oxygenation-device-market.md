<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Veno Artrial (VA)</li><li>Veno Venous (VV)</li><li>Artrio Venous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) device market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. Veno Artrial (VA): This type supports both heart and lung functions by diverting the blood from a vein to an artery, oxygenating it, and pumping it back into the arterial system.</p><p>2. Veno Venous (VV): VV ECMO assists only the lung function by oxygenating the blood and returning it back to the veins, allowing the heart to pump it through the body.</p><p>3. Artrio Venous (AV): AV ECMO involves diverting blood from an artery to a vein, facilitating both heart and lung support. These different types cater to specific medical conditions, offering life-sustaining support to critically ill patients.</p></p>
