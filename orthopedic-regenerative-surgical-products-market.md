<p><p>The orthopedic regenerative surgical products market includes various types of products such as allografts, synthetic materials, cell-based therapies, and viscosupplements. Allografts are tissues obtained from a compatible donor, while synthetic materials are artificially created materials used to replace damaged tissues. Cell-based therapies involve using stem cells or other cellular components to regenerate damaged tissues. Viscosupplements are injectable substances used to lubricate and cushion joints affected by osteoarthritis. These different types of products offer treatment options for various orthopedic conditions, promoting tissue regeneration and alleviating pain.</p></p>
