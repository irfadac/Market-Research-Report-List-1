<p><p>The wet system valves market includes two main types: Flanged Type and Grooved Type. Flanged Type valves are designed with flanges on both ends, allowing them to be bolted directly to flanged pipes or fittings. This ensures a secure and leak-free connection. On the other hand, Grooved Type valves have grooves on their ends, which allow them to be quickly and easily joined using grooved couplings. These valves are known for their simplicity and efficiency in installation, making them popular in various industries.</p></p>
