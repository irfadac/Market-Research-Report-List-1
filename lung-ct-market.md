<p><p>Lung CT market types refer to different types of computed tomography (CT) scans used specifically for examining the lungs. These machines use a spiral scanning technique to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the lungs. Each CT type offers varying levels of image quality, speed, and radiation dose.
