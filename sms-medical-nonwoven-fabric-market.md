<p><p>SMS Medical Nonwoven Fabric is a type of nonwoven fabric specifically designed for use in medical and healthcare applications. SMS stands for Spunbond Meltblown Spunbond, which is a manufacturing process that involves two layers of spunbonded polypropylene fabric being sandwiched with a layer of meltblown polypropylene fabric. This combination provides the fabric with excellent barrier properties, strength, and durability, making it ideal for use in various medical products such as surgical gowns, masks, drapes, and protective clothing.</p><p>The SMS Medical Nonwoven Fabric Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
