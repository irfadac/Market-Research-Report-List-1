<p><p>The Patient Lift market comprises two main types: Electric Patient Lift and Conventional Patient Lift. Electric Patient Lifts utilize electrically powered systems to lift and transfer patients safely. These devices offer enhanced maneuverability and reduce the strain on caregivers. On the other hand, Conventional Patient Lifts are manually operated, relying on the physical strength of caregivers for patient transfer. While they may require more effort from the user, conventional lifts are often a more cost-effective option. Both types aim to facilitate the movement and transfer of patients, promoting safety and comfort during medical procedures or mobility support.</p></p>
