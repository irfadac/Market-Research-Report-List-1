The growing geriatric population, which is more prone to dental problems, also contributes to market growth.</p><p>Technological advancements in dental filling instruments have led to the development of more efficient and precise tools. This has positively influenced the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, there is a growing trend towards the use of tooth-colored composite fillings instead of traditional amalgam fillings. Composite fillings provide aesthetic advantages as they match the natural color of teeth, and they also have better adhesive properties. The increasing demand for composite fillings is driving the growth of the dental filling instruments market.</p><p>In addition, the rising focus on preventive dentistry and minimally invasive techniques is also fueling market growth. Dentists are shifting towards preventive measures such as early diagnosis and minimal tooth preparation, leading to increased demand for dental filling instruments.</p><p>Overall, the dental filling instruments market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years due to the increasing prevalence of dental diseases, technological advancements, and shifting trends towards preventive dentistry and aesthetic fillings.</p></p>
