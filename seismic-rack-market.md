<p><p>The seismic rack market consists of three main types: double column, four-column, and six-column seismic racks. Double column seismic racks are designed to provide stability and support during seismic events, with two columns offering increased structural integrity. Four-column seismic racks provide even greater stability by incorporating four columns for enhanced resistance to seismic forces. Six-column seismic racks are the most robust option, as they utilize six columns to ensure maximum protection and safety for equipment housed within the rack. Overall, these different types cater to varying load capacities and seismic requirements.</p></p>
