<p><p>The electric golf trolley market can be categorized into two types: lead acid and lithium. Lead acid batteries have been traditionally used in electric golf trolleys, offering a cost-effective solution. However, they are heavier and bulkier compared to lithium batteries, which provide a lighter and more compact alternative. While lead acid batteries are cheaper and provide longer runtime, lithium batteries have a higher energy density, enabling longer lifespan, faster charging, and reduced weight. Both options cater to different consumer preferences, with lead acid targeting budget-conscious buyers and lithium appealing to those seeking advanced features and convenience.</p></p>
