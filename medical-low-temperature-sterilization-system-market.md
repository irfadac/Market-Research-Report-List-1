<p><p>The Medical Low Temperature Sterilization System Market includes three main types: Ethylene Oxide (ETO) Sterilization, Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization, and Plasma Sterilization. Ethylene Oxide (ETO) Sterilization involves using a gas to kill microorganisms, making it suitable for heat-sensitive equipment. Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization utilizes a vaporized hydrogen peroxide solution to achieve sterilization, offering a safer alternative to ETO. Plasma Sterilization employs low-temperature plasma to eliminate bacteria and other pathogens effectively. These systems play a crucial role in maintaining sterile conditions in medical facilities.</p></p>
