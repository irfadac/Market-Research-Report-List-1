<p><strong>The UV Nanoimprint Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Layer</li><li>Sacrificial Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV Nanoimprint Resin market includes two types: Permanent Layer and Sacrificial Layer. Permanent Layer resin is used to create a permanent pattern on the substrate by curing the resin under UV light, providing long-lasting durability. On the other hand, Sacrificial Layer resin is used to transfer a pattern temporarily onto the substrate, which can be easily removed after the imprinting process. These two types cater to different needs in the market, offering flexibility and versatility in nanoimprint technology applications.</p></p>
