<p><p>The Explosive Trace Detection (ETD) market includes three types: handheld, ground-mounted, and vehicle-mounted systems. Handheld ETD systems are portable and allow for quick, on-the-spot detection of explosive traces. Ground-mounted systems are stationary devices typically used at security checkpoints to scan luggage, packages, or people for explosive residue. Vehicle-mounted systems are installed on vehicles such as cars or trucks, enabling mobile detection and screening of large areas or transportation networks. These different types of ETD systems cater to varying security needs and environments.</p></p>
