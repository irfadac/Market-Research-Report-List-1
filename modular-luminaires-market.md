<p><p>Modular luminaires refer to lighting fixtures that are designed in modular units, allowing for flexibility and customization in their arrangement and configuration. The market for modular luminaires can be categorized into different types based on the light source technology used. LED luminaires utilize light-emitting diodes to provide efficient and long-lasting illumination. CFL luminaires use compact fluorescent lamps, which are energy-efficient and produce warm light. LFL luminaires employ linear fluorescent lamps, commonly known as tube lights. Lastly, the "Others" category includes any other light source technologies used in modular luminaires, such as incandescent bulbs or halogen lamps.</p></p>
