<p><p>Pressure operated piston valve market includes various types such as 2-way valve, 3-way valve, 4-way valve, and others. A 2-way valve has two openings for the fluid to flow in and out, allowing for simple on/off control. A 3-way valve has three openings, providing more advanced control options, such as diverting or mixing fluids. A 4-way valve, on the other hand, has four openings, enabling complex flow patterns and applications in pneumatic and hydraulic systems. The "others" category comprises valve types that do not fit into the aforementioned categories but still serve specific purposes in the market.</p></p>
