<p><p>Industrial Robot Sensors are essential components of industrial robots that enable them to perceive and interact with their environment. There are various types of sensors used in the market. Vision sensors are responsible for capturing and processing visual information. Force Torque sensors measure forces and torques applied during interactions. Temperature sensors monitor the temperature of the robot's components. Pressure sensors detect changes in pressure. Position sensors provide precise positional feedback. Finally, there are other types of sensors that can include proximity sensors, optical sensors, or accelerometer sensors, depending on specific robot requirements.</p></p>
