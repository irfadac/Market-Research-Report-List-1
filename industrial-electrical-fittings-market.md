<p><p>Industrial electrical fittings refer to various components used in electrical installations to connect, protect, and terminate electrical wires and cables. There are two main types of industrial electrical fittings: metallic electrical fittings and non-metallic electrical fittings. Metallic fittings are made from conductive materials like steel or aluminum, providing durability and excellent electrical conductivity. Non-metallic fittings are made from insulating materials such as PVC or nylon and are preferred for their corrosion resistance and lighter weight. Both types are crucial in ensuring safe and reliable electrical connections in industrial settings.</p></p>
