<p><p>Tricone drill bits are indispensable tools used in the oil and gas industry for drilling boreholes. There are two main types of tricone drill bits in the market: TCI (Tungsten Carbide Insert) drill bits and milled tooth drill bits. TCI drill bits feature durable tungsten carbide buttons that are strategically placed on the cones to effectively crush hard rock formations. On the other hand, milled tooth drill bits use steel teeth that are carefully milled onto the cones to provide efficient drilling in softer formations. Both types play crucial roles in the oil and gas drilling process.</p></p>
