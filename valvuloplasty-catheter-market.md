<p><p>A valvuloplasty catheter is a medical device used in the treatment of heart valve diseases. It is a type of balloon catheter that is inserted into a blood vessel and guided to the heart valves. This procedure helps improve blood flow and alleviate symptoms associated with heart valve diseases.</p><p>The valvuloplasty catheter market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period.
