<p><strong>The Compostable Cutlery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PLA</li><li>CPLA</li><li>Starch Blends</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compostable cutlery market consists of three main types: PLA, CPLA, and starch blends. PLA (Polylactic Acid) is derived from renewable resources like corn starch and sugarcane. It is biodegradable and has a relatively low impact on the environment. CPLA (Crystallized Polylactic Acid) is a modified form of PLA that undergoes crystallization for improved heat resistance. Starch blends are made from a mixture of different plant-based starches and other biodegradable materials. They offer a cost-effective and eco-friendly alternative to traditional plastic cutlery. Overall, these types of compostable cutlery promote sustainability and reduce waste.</p></p>
