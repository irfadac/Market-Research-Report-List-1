<p><ul><li>Monofilament</li><li>Coiled</li><li>Coated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endoscopy Guidewire market includes three main types: Monofilament, Coiled, and Coated wires. Monofilament wires consist of a single strand and are used for basic procedures and easy navigation. The Coiled wires have a spiral design, providing more support and flexibility for complex procedures. These wires are used in difficult anatomies and tight spaces. Coated wires have a smooth outer layer, reducing friction and allowing for smoother navigation through blood vessels or other narrow passages. They are commonly used in delicate procedures, such as cardiac interventions.</p></p>
