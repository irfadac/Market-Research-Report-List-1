<p><p>The high voltage circuit breaker market can be categorized into various types. Vacuum high voltage circuit breakers use a vacuum to extinguish the arc when the circuit is broken. Sulfur Hexafluoride high voltage circuit breakers use SF6 gas to extinguish the arc. Air high voltage circuit breakers utilize compressed air to disrupt the circuit. Oil high voltage circuit breakers use oil as an insulating medium. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses alternative technologies or hybrid circuit breakers that may use a combination of these methods or different materials.</p></p>
