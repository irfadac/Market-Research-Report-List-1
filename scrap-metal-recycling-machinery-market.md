<p><p>Scrap metal recycling machinery market encompasses a wide range of equipment types. Baler Press machines are used to compact and bind scrap metals into bales for easy transportation and storage. Shredders are designed to break down bulky and large pieces of metal into smaller fragments, facilitating further processing. Granulators, on the other hand, transform these smaller metal chips into granules for recycling. Shears are specialized in cutting and shaping scrap metals. Separators help in separating different types of metals from the scrap material. Lastly, the market also includes other machinery types that serve various functions in the scrap metal recycling process.</p></p>
