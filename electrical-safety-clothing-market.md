<p><p>Electrical safety clothing, also known as arc flash clothing, is protective clothing designed to protect workers from electric arc flashes and electric shock hazards. Additionally, there is a rising demand for customized safety clothing that meets specific industry standards and requirements.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the adoption of smart technologies in electrical safety clothing.
