<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column EPS，C-EPS</li><li>Pinion EPS，P-EPS</li><li>Rack EPS，R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automobile Electric Power Steering (EPS) market comprises various types of systems. Column EPS utilizes a column-mounted electric motor and is often found in high-end vehicles. C-EPS refers to Column EPS with an added controller, enhancing safety and performance. Pinion EPS incorporates a pinion gear system, allowing for more direct steering feel. P-EPS stands for Pump EPS, where an electric pump assists the steering system. Rack EPS features an electric motor attached to the rack housing, providing precise control. Lastly, R-EPS refers to Rack EPS with a simplified structure for better cost-efficiency.</p></p>
