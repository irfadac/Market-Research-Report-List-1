<p><p>The Sample Cutter market comprises three main types: Manual, Pneumatic, and Electric. Manual cutters are operated by hand, providing basic cutting functions and requiring physical effort. Pneumatic cutters utilize compressed air for cutting, allowing for increased precision and efficiency. They require an external air compressor to run. Electric cutters are powered by electricity, offering enhanced cutting capabilities and ease of use. They may be corded or cordless, and provide faster and more consistent cutting performance. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
