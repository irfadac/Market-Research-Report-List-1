<p><p>Portable water hardness meters are devices used to measure the concentration of minerals, primarily calcium and magnesium ions, in water. These meters are divided into two types based on the concentration of minerals they can detect. Low concentration water hardness meters are designed to measure minerals in water with a low mineral content, typical of soft water sources. On the other hand, high concentration water hardness meters are specifically calibrated to accurately measure the abundance of minerals in water sources with high mineral content, such as hard water.</p></p>
