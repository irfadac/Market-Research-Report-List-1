These are suitable for applications where only one signal needs to be processed.</p><p>2 Channel Type op amps have the capability to amplify or process two input signals simultaneously. They are commonly used in audio applications or applications requiring signal mixing.</p><p>4 Channel Type op amps are designed to handle four input signals at once. These are often used in multi-channel audio systems or complex data acquisition systems.</p></p>
