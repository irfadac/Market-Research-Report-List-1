Firstly, an increasing number of surgeries being performed globally, coupled with the growing concern for infection control and prevention, is driving the demand for reusable surgical gowns. <p><p>The reusable surgical gown market offers different types of materials, including PP non-woven material, SMS non-woven material, and others.
