<p><p>The basin mixer tap market can be categorized into different types. Standard basin mixer taps are the most common and operate by manually adjusting the hot and cold water flow. Self-closing basin mixer taps shut off the water flow after a certain period of use to conserve water. Thermostatic basin mixer taps maintain a constant water temperature, protecting users from sudden temperature changes.
