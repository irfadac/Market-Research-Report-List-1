<p><p>A Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC) is a specialized type of integrated circuit that is designed to perform functions related to radio frequency signals. The key drivers for this growth include the increasing demand for wireless devices and the rising adoption of 5G technology. With the growing number of smartphones, tablets, and other wireless consumer devices, the demand for RFICs is expected to surge.
