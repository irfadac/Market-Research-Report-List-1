<p><p>The Aerospace Data Acquisition System market consists of various types of data recorders that play a crucial role in aviation safety. Flight Data Recorders (FDR) are capable of capturing flight parameters like altitude, speed, and heading. Cockpit Data Recorders (CDR) record essential data from instruments and controls in the cockpit. Voyage Data Recorders (VDR) are specialized for maritime vessels and gather information such as position, speed, and navigation data. Other data recorders include Engine Data Recorders or Quick Access Recorders, which monitor specific engine performance and general aircraft data.</p></p>
