<p><p>The Skin Cleansing Machine market includes two main types: High Frequency Vibration Cleaning and Adsorption Cleaning. High Frequency Vibration Cleaning utilizes rapid vibrations to cleanse the skin by dislodging dirt, oil, and impurities from deep within the pores. It is effective in removing dead skin cells and improving blood circulation. On the other hand, Adsorption Cleaning uses suction power to remove dirt, oil, and blackheads from the skin's surface. It helps in unclogging pores and providing a deep cleanse. Both types offer different approaches to skin cleansing, catering to different skincare needs.</p></p>
