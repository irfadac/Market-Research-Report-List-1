<p><p>The static material testing machine market is segmented into two major types: electromechanical type and hydraulic type. The electromechanical type machines use an electric motor and screw system to exert force on a specimen to test its mechanical properties. These machines are known for their accuracy, versatility, and ease of use. On the other hand, hydraulic type machines use hydraulic power to apply force on the specimen. They are typically used for high-capacity or high-force applications and are preferred for their ability to generate large force levels.</p></p>
