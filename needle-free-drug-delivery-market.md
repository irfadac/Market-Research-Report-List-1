<p><p>Needle-free drug delivery refers to the administration of medication into the body without using traditional needles. Jet injectors use high-pressure streams of liquid medication to penetrate the skin without the need for a needle. Transdermal patches deliver medication through the skin using a patch or an adhesive. These technologies provide efficient drug delivery, reduce the risk of needlestick injuries, and enhance patient comfort.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the development of microneedle-based drug delivery systems.
