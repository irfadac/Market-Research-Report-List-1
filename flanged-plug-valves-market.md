<p><p>Flanged plug valves refer to a type of valves used for controlling the flow of fluids in pipelines. In terms of market types, there are three variations available. Full port plug valves have a larger orifice, enabling maximum flow capacity, while standard port plug valves have a smaller orifice, reducing flow capacity. Round port plug valves have a single circular hole and are often used for smaller flow control applications. Each market type caters to different flow requirements, ensuring efficient and effective fluid regulation in various industries.</p></p>
