<p><strong>The Wireless Broadband Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point-to-Point (PTP)</li><li>Point-to-Multipoint (PMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless broadband solutions are classified into two main market types: Point-to-Point (PTP) and Point-to-Multipoint (PMP). In a PTP market, wireless connectivity is established between two specific locations, allowing for direct communication. This type of solution is commonly used in scenarios where a dedicated and private connection is required, such as connecting two buildings within a campus. On the other hand, in a PMP market, wireless connectivity is established between one central location and multiple remote locations, enabling efficient and cost-effective communication for a larger number of users. This is commonly used in scenarios like providing internet access to a neighborhood or community.</p></p>
