<p><p>Damp proof mats, also known as moisture barrier mats, are specialized mats or membranes designed to prevent the penetration of moisture into buildings. They act as a protective layer, preventing the transfer of moisture from the ground to the interior of the building.</p><p>The future outlook for the damp proof mats market is positive, with a projected growth rate of 9.8% during the forecast period.
