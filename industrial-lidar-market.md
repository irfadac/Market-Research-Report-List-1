<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Industrial LiDAR market includes two main types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR uses solid-state components like semiconductors to emit and receive laser pulses, making it more compact, reliable, and cost-effective compared to traditional mechanical systems. It's suitable for various applications, including autonomous vehicles and drones. On the other hand, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating or oscillating mirrors to steer laser beams, providing a 360-degree view but at higher costs and larger sizes. It is used in industries like forestry and mining for 3D mapping and object detection.</p></p>
