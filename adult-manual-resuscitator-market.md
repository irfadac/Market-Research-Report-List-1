<p><p>An adult manual resuscitator, also known as an Ambu bag or bag-valve-mask (BVM), is a handheld device used to manually provide positive pressure ventilation to individuals who are experiencing respiratory distress or failure. It consists of a self-inflating bag connected to a face mask or endotracheal tube, allowing the user to deliver oxygen and fill the lungs with air.</p><p>The adult manual resuscitator market is witnessing steady growth and is expected to expand at a CAGR of 6.30% during the forecast period. This market growth can be attributed to various factors like the rising incidences of respiratory emergencies, an increasing number of surgeries and critical care procedures, and the growing geriatric population susceptible to respiratory illnesses. Additionally, the demand for manual resuscitators has surged due to the outbreak of COVID-19, as these devices play a crucial role in supporting the breathing function of patients suffering from severe respiratory complications.</p><p>Technological advancements in adult manual resuscitators have also contributed to market growth. Manufacturers are introducing innovative features like pressure relief valves, adjustable flow rates, ergonomic designs, and improved functionality to enhance patient comfort and ease of use for healthcare professionals.
