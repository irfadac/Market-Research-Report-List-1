<p><p>The LED (Light Emitting Diode) supply chain refers to the network of processes involved in the manufacturing, distribution, and retailing of LED products. The integration of LED lighting with smart home automation systems and the increasing popularity of smart city initiatives are driving the demand for intelligent LED lighting solutions.</p><p>Moreover, the market is also witnessing the emergence of innovative LED packaging technologies to enhance the performance and efficiency of LED products.
