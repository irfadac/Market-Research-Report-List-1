<p><p>The automatic tridimensional storehouse market offers various types of systems for storage and retrieval. Unit Load systems are designed for handling large quantities of goods. Mini Load systems are suitable for smaller items and high-speed operations. Vertical Lift Module (VLM) systems use an automated crane to retrieve goods stored in a vertical structure. Carousel systems consist of rotating shelves, enabling efficient item retrieval. Mid Load systems combine the advantages of Unit Load and Mini Load systems. Autostore market offers dense and compact storage with robots accessing bins stacked on top of each other.</p></p>
