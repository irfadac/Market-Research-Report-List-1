<p><p>Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor Sterilization Equipment Market Types refer to two different methods used for decontamination purposes. Chamber Decontamination involves using a closed chamber or enclosure to sterilize medical equipment, laboratory tools, or other sensitive materials using hydrogen peroxide vapor. On the other hand, Room Decontamination involves the use of hydrogen peroxide vapor to sterilize an entire room or area, including all surfaces and equipment present.
