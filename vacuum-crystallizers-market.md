<p><p>Vacuum crystallizers are classified based on technology type and mounting type. In terms of technology type, there are continuous and batch vacuum crystallizers. Continuous vacuum crystallizers ensure a continuous flow of the liquid to be crystallized, while batch vacuum crystallizers process a fixed quantity of liquid at a time. Regarding mounting type, vacuum crystallizers can be vertical or horizontal. Vertical vacuum crystallizers have a vertically aligned structure, while horizontal vacuum crystallizers have a horizontally aligned structure. These classifications cater to varying operational preferences and requirements in the vacuum crystallizers market.</p></p>
