<p><strong>The E-passport Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biometrics</li><li>Radio-Frequency Identification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-passport technology market consists of two main types: biometrics and radio-frequency identification (RFID). Biometric e-passports utilize unique physical or behavioral characteristics of individuals, such as fingerprints or iris scans, for identification purposes. This helps ensure secure authentication and reduces the risk of identity fraud. On the other hand, RFID e-passports use radio waves for wireless communication between the passport and reader devices, enabling quick and contactless identification. Both biometrics and RFID technologies enhance the efficiency and reliability of passport authentication processes, ensuring smoother and more secure travel experiences for individuals.</p></p>
