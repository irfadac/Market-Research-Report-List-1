<p><strong>The Jet Aerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compressed Air Type</li><li>Self-Aspirating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet aerators are a type of technology widely used in wastewater treatment and aeration processes. Compressed air type jet aerators operate by injecting compressed air into the water, creating a high-velocity jet stream that enhances oxygen transfer and promotes mixing. On the other hand, self-aspirating type jet aerators utilize the velocity of water flow to draw in surrounding air, creating a suction effect and ensuring efficient oxygen supply. Both types of jet aerators contribute to effective wastewater treatment by increasing dissolved oxygen levels and improving the overall quality of water bodies.</p></p>
