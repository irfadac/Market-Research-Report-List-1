<p><p>Eye examination equipment market consists of various types of equipment used for diagnosing and measuring visual abilities. Phoropter is a commonly used device that allows optometrists to test patients' refractive errors and prescribe corrective lenses. Autorefractors provide automated measurement of refractive errors and are particularly useful for non-verbal or uncooperative patients. Lensmeters are used to measure parameters of eyeglass lenses, such as power and astigmatism. Other equipment includes slit lamps for examining the front of the eye, ophthalmoscopes for evaluating the retina, and tonometers for measuring eye pressure.</p></p>
