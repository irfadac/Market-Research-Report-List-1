<p><strong>The Automatic Bird Feeders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cage Automatic Bird Feeders</li><li>Open Automatic Bird Feeders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic bird feeders market can be categorized into two main types: Cage Automatic Bird Feeders and Open Automatic Bird Feeders. Cage automatic bird feeders are designed with a cage surrounding the feeding area, preventing larger birds or squirrels from accessing the food, thus attracting smaller birds. On the other hand, open automatic bird feeders do not have any physical barriers, allowing all types of birds, including larger ones, to freely access the food. These different types cater to different preferences and needs of bird watchers and aim to attract specific bird species.</p></p>
