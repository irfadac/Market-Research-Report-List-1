<p><p>The wireless tower market is composed of three main types: three tube wireless towers, angle-steel wireless towers, and guyed wireless towers. A three tube wireless tower is a structure made of three cylindrical tubes, providing stability and durability for mounting antennas. Angle-steel wireless towers are constructed using angled steel sections, offering cost-effectiveness and easy installation. Guyed wireless towers feature guy wires that support the structure, making them suitable for areas with high wind speeds. Each market segment caters to different needs and preferences in terms of design, functionality, and budget.</p></p>
