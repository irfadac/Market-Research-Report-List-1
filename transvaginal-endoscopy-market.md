<p><p>Transvaginal endoscopy market comprises flexible and rigid endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are minimally invasive devices with a long, thin, and flexible tube that enables easy maneuverability and visualization of the internal reproductive organs. They are generally preferred for diagnostic procedures due to their ability to provide a detailed examination of the vagina and cervix. On the other hand, rigid endoscopes have a fixed shape and are commonly used for surgical interventions, such as tissue removal or laser procedures, as they offer better control and stability during the operation. Both types contribute to the evolving field of transvaginal endoscopy.</p></p>
