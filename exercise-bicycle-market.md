<p><p>The exercise bicycle market consists of two main types: upright exercise bicycles and recumbent exercise bicycles. Upright exercise bicycles resemble traditional bicycles, with the user sitting upright and pedaling in a forward-leaning position. They are compact and provide a more intense workout targeting core and upper body muscles. On the other hand, recumbent exercise bicycles have a reclined seat and offer a more comfortable workout experience, ideal for those with back or joint issues. They provide a lower-impact workout for the lower body muscles. Both types cater to different preferences and fitness goals.</p></p>
