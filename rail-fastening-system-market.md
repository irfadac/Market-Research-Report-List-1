The Rail Spike Series includes metal spikes hammered into wooden sleepers to secure the rails. The Track Bolt Series comprises bolts that are used to connect rail segments and fasten them to sleepers. The Rail Clip Series consists of clips that clamp the rail to the baseplate. Other types of fasteners include elastic clips, fishplates, and baseplates. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring the stability and safety of railway tracks.</p></p>
