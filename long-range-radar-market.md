<p><p>The Long Range Radar market includes various types of radar systems used for different purposes. Surveillance and Airborne Early Warning radar is used for detecting and tracking airborne threats over large distances. Tracking and Fire Control radar is used for accurate targeting of weapons and tracking fast-moving objects. Multi-function radar combines multiple capabilities such as surveillance, tracking, and weather monitoring. Synthetic Aperture and Moving Target Indicator radar provides high-resolution imaging and moving target detection. Weapon Locating radar and C-RAM systems are used for locating enemy artillery and defending against rocket attacks. Ground Penetrating radar is used for subsurface imaging and detection. Weather radar is employed for monitoring and forecasting weather patterns.</p></p>
