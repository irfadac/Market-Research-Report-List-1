<p><ul><li>Testing Equipment Before Packaging</li><li>Testing Equipment After Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor testing equipment refers to the devices and instruments used to assess the functionality and quality of semiconductor components. There are two main types: testing equipment before packaging and testing equipment after packaging. Testing equipment before packaging is utilized to evaluate the components' performance, electrical characteristics, and functionality before they are assembled into packages. On the other hand, testing equipment after packaging is used to test the final packaged units for any defects, such as shorts, open circuits, or malfunctions. Both types of testing equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and performance of semiconductor components.</p></p>
