<p><p>Immunochromato Test Readers are used to analyze immunochromatographic tests. There are two main market types for these readers: the Absorption Method and the Fluorescence Method. In the Absorption Method, the reader measures the color intensity of the test line and compares it to a control line to determine the concentration of the target analyte. In the Fluorescence Method, the reader uses a fluorescent label on the test line, which emits light when exposed to a specific wavelength, allowing for accurate detection and quantification of the analyte.</p></p>
