Object Square Telephoto Lens is a type of telecentric lens that enables imaging of square-shaped objects with high precision and minimal distortion. It is suitable for applications involving object analysis or inspection. Bi-Telecentric Lens, on the other hand, offers telecentric imaging from both the object and the image side. This dual telecentricity reduces measurement errors caused by varying object distances and angles, making it ideal for precise dimensional measurements.</p></p>
