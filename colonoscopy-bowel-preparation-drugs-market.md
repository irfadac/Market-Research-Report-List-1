<p><p>The colonoscopy bowel preparation drugs market includes various types of drugs used to clean the colon before the procedure. Isosmotic agents maintain an equal amount of fluid inside and outside the colon, ensuring a balanced concentration. Hyposmotic agents increase the amount of fluid inside the colon, resulting in a watery stool. Hyperosmotic agents draw fluids into the colon, causing a laxative effect. Combination agents typically combine different types of drugs to achieve optimal bowel cleansing. These drugs are essential for effective colonoscopy preparation and contribute to the growth of the market.</p></p>
