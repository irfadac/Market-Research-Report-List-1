<p><p>The Marine Watertight Doors market comprises two main types: Sliding Watertight Doors and Hinged Watertight Doors. Sliding Watertight Doors are designed to slide horizontally, providing a secure seal against water under high pressure. They are commonly used in bulkheads and compartments where space is limited. On the other hand, Hinged Watertight Doors open and close on a vertical hinge, creating an effective barrier against water ingress. These doors are often installed in areas with larger openings, such as ship entrances or engine rooms. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of maritime vessels.</p></p>
