<p><p>The beverage carriers market is divided into two main types: semi-rigid and rigid. Semi-rigid beverage carriers are made from flexible materials, such as cardboard or paperboard, and have some level of structural support. They are usually used for carrying smaller quantities of beverages, such as six-packs of cans or bottles. On the other hand, rigid beverage carriers are made from more durable materials, like plastic or metal, and offer strong protection and stability. They are commonly used for carrying larger quantities of beverages, such as cases of drinks or party trays.</p></p>
