<p><p>High Purity Photoacid Generator (PAG) market comprises two main types of initiators - Photo-Radical Initiators and Photo-Cationic Initiators. Photo-Radical Initiators are chemicals that undergo a photochemical reaction to form free radicals, which can initiate polymerization processes. On the other hand, Photo-Cationic Initiators generate cations upon exposure to light, which can initiate polymerization reactions. Both types of initiators are essential in industries such as electronics, semiconductors, and coatings for their ability to control the polymerization process with high purity and precision.</p></p>
