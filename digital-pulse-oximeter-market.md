<p><p>Digital pulse oximeters are medical devices used to measure the oxygen saturation levels in a person's blood. LED digital pulse oximeters utilize light-emitting diodes to emit and detect light, providing accurate readings. On the other hand, LCD digital pulse oximeters use liquid crystal displays to display the readings. Both types of products offer the same functionality but differ in terms of the technology used to present the results. LED digital pulse oximeters are popular due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while LCD digital pulse oximeters provide a clearer and more detailed display.</p></p>
