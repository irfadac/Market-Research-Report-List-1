<p><p>The smart game phone market types can be categorized into two cooling methods: water cooling and air cooling. Water cooling utilizes a closed-loop system with a built-in liquid-filled heat pipe to dissipate heat generated during intensive gaming sessions. This method ensures efficient heat transfer and helps in maintaining optimal phone temperature. On the other hand, air cooling involves the use of fans or external cooling systems to regulate the phone's temperature. Both methods are designed to prevent overheating and provide users with an enhanced gaming experience.</p></p>
