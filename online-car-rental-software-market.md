<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Online car rental software market is classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software refers to a system hosted on the internet by a third-party provider, which allows users to access and manage their car rental operations remotely using any device with internet connectivity. On the other hand, on-premises software involves installing the software on servers and infrastructure owned and maintained by the car rental company themselves, requiring them to manage and maintain the system internally on their premises. Both types offer different advantages and cater to different preferences and requirements of car rental businesses.</p></p>
