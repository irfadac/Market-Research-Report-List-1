<p><strong>The Food Delivery Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aggregators</li><li>New Delivery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food delivery service market types can be classified as aggregators and new delivery markets. Aggregators are platforms that connect customers and restaurants, allowing customers to order food from various restaurants in one place. They act as intermediaries, processing orders and delivering them through the restaurants or independent drivers. On the other hand, new delivery markets are emerging platforms where the delivery service is provided directly by the platform itself. They may have their own fleet of drivers, ensuring faster and more efficient food delivery to customers.</p></p>
