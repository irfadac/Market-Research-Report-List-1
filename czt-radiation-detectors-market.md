<p><p>CZT radiation detectors are used to measure and detect radiation levels in various industries. There are different types of CZT detectors available in the market. Planar type detectors consist of a single electrode on one side, while the opposite side is divided into individual pixels. Coplanar grid detectors have a grid of electrodes on one side, allowing for better energy resolution. Other market types include strip detectors, which have long, thin electrodes, and 3D detectors, which have an array of pixels on both sides. These different types cater to varying needs and applications in radiation detection.</p></p>
