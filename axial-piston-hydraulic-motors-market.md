<p><ul><li>Swash Plate</li><li>Bent Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axial piston hydraulic motors are used in various industries for converting hydraulic power into rotational mechanical power. There are two types: swash plate and bent axis. Swash plate motors use a rotating swash plate to vary the angle of the pistons, allowing for different displacements and speeds. Bent axis motors have pistons that are mounted at an angle to the drive shaft, resulting in a bent axis of rotation. Both types provide high efficiency, compact size, and a wide range of torque and speed options to suit different application requirements in hydraulic systems.</p></p>
