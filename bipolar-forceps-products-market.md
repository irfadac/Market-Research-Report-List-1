Bipolar forceps consist of two tips that capture the tissue between them and deliver the electric current, ensuring minimal damage to surrounding tissues.</p><p>The global bipolar forceps products market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
