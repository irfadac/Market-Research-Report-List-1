<p><p>The Electric Cream Separator market can be categorized into two types - hermetic type and non-hermetic type. Hermetic type cream separators are designed to be airtight, preventing any leakage or contamination. They are commonly used in industries where hygiene and precision are crucial, such as dairy and food processing. On the other hand, non-hermetic type separators do not have an airtight seal and are generally used in less critical applications. These types of separators are often more cost-effective and can be found in smaller-scale operations or home use.</p></p>
