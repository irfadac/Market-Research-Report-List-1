<p><ul><li>Thermocouples</li><li>Thermistors</li><li>Resistive Temperature Detectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable temperature calibrators are devices essential for accuracy verification and calibration of temperature measurement instrumentation in various industries. There are different types available in the market, such as thermocouples, thermistors, resistive temperature detectors (RTDs), and others. Thermocouples rely on the principle of voltage generated from the junction of two different metals when exposed to varying temperatures. Thermistors work based on the change in electrical resistance with temperature. RTDs use the change in electrical resistance of pure metals as temperature changes. The "others" category includes alternative technologies for temperature measurement and calibration. Overall, these calibrators cater to diverse temperature measurement needs for utmost precision in different industries.</p></p>
