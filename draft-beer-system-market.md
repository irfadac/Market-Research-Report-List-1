<p><strong>The Draft Beer System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Draw</li><li>Long-Draw</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Draft Beer System market consists of three main types: Direct-Draw, Long-Draw, and Air Cooled. Direct-Draw systems are typically used in small establishments where the kegs are stored near the tap, allowing for a short distance for the beer to travel. Long-Draw systems are used in larger establishments, where the kegs are stored far away from the tap and the beer needs to travel a long distance through insulated lines. Air Cooled systems use fans or blowers to maintain the temperature of the beer, making them suitable for establishments with limited refrigeration options.</p></p>
