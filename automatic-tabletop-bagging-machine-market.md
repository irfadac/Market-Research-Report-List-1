<p><p>The automatic tabletop bagging machine market can be classified into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines require minimal human involvement as they can handle the entire bagging process independently, including bag opening, filling, and sealing. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of manual input, such as bag loading and sealing. While fully automatic machines offer higher productivity and efficiency, semi-automatic machines are more cost-effective and suitable for smaller-scale operations.</p></p>
