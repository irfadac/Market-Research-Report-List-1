<p><p>The mobile attendance tracking system market is classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions store attendance data on remote servers, allowing users to access it from any device via the internet. This type offers flexibility and scalability, making it suitable for businesses of all sizes. On-premises systems store attendance data on local servers, requiring physical infrastructure and maintenance. This type provides greater control and security but may involve higher costs and limited accessibility. Both types serve the purpose of accurately tracking and managing employee attendance.</p></p>
