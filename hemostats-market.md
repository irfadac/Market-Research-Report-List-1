<p><p>Hemostats are medical devices used to control bleeding during surgeries or other medical procedures. There are various types of hemostats available in the market. Thrombin-based hemostats, oxidized regenerated cellulose based hemostats, combination hemostats, gelatin based hemostats, and collagen based hemostats are the different categories. Thrombin-based hemostats contain thrombin, a protein that helps in clot formation. Oxidized regenerated cellulose based hemostats contain a substance that promotes blood clotting. Combination hemostats combine various ingredients for effective bleeding control. Gelatin based hemostats consist of a gelatin matrix that aids in clotting. Collagen based hemostats utilize collagen, a natural protein, to enhance clot formation.</p></p>
