<p><p>The intravenous filter market has valuable applications in the treatment of pulmonary embolism and venous thrombosis. Pulmonary embolism refers to the blockage of blood vessels in the lungs, while venous thrombosis involves the formation of blood clots in the veins. Intravenous filters are used in both cases to prevent these emboli or clots from reaching vital organs, such as the heart or brain. By capturing and trapping these potentially dangerous clots, intravenous filters aid in the prevention of life-threatening conditions and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
