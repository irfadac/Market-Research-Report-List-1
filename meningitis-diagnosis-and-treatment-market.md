<p><p>Meningitis is an inflammation of the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord, usually caused by an infection. Early diagnosis and treatment are critical as it can potentially lead to severe complications and even death. The diagnosis of meningitis typically involves a combination of physical examination, analysis of patient history, and various laboratory tests, including blood cultures, spinal fluid analysis, and imaging tests. Treatment for meningitis usually involves the administration of antibiotics or antiviral medications, depending on the cause.</p><p>The global meningitis diagnosis and treatment market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period.
