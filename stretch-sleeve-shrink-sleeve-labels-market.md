<p><p>Stretch sleeve and shrink sleeve labels are types of packaging labels that are widely used in the packaging industry. These labels are made of flexible plastic materials, such as PVC, PETG, or OPS, which can be stretched or shrunken to fit the shape of the container. </p><p>Stretch sleeve labels are applied to containers using specialized machinery that stretches the label over the container. These labels are mainly used for products that are cylindrical in shape, such as bottles and jars. Shrink sleeve labels, on the other hand, are applied to containers using heat. When heat is applied, the label shrinks and conforms tightly to the shape of the product container, providing a 360-degree coverage.</p><p>The global stretch sleeve and shrink sleeve labels market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for visually appealing and innovative packaging solutions, coupled with the growth of the food and beverage industry, has been driving the market growth.
