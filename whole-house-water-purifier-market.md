<p><p>A whole house water purifier, also known as a point-of-entry water treatment system, is a filtration system designed to remove impurities and contaminants from the water supply of an entire house. Manufacturers are now incorporating sensors, connectivity features, and mobile applications to enable real-time monitoring and control of the purifiers. This allows users to monitor water quality, filter status, and receive notifications or alerts when maintenance or filter replacement is required.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the preference for eco-friendly and sustainable water purifiers. Many consumers are opting for systems that utilize natural or chemical-free filtration methods.
