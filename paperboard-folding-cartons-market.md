<p><p>The paperboard folding cartons market consists of two main types: standard cartons and aseptic cartons. Standard cartons are commonly used for packaging various products such as food, beverages, healthcare, and personal care items. They are typically made from virgin or recycled paperboard and are designed to provide protection, visibility, and easy handling. On the other hand, aseptic cartons are specifically designed to maintain the sterility and freshness of perishable products by using multiple layers of barrier materials. They are primarily used for packaging dairy products, juices, and other beverages that require a longer shelf life.</p></p>
