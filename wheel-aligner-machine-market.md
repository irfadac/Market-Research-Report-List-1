<p><p>The Wheel Aligner Machine market consists of two primary types: CCD Wheel Aligner and 3D Wheel Aligner. CCD Wheel Aligner utilizes Charge-Coupled Device technology to measure the wheel alignment angles accurately. It is widely used in various automotive applications due to its compact size and simple operation. On the other hand, the 3D Wheel Aligner employs three-dimensional imaging technology to provide precise and comprehensive wheel alignment measurements. This advanced system offers enhanced accuracy, faster alignment process, and the ability to diagnose complex alignment issues, resulting in improved performance and safety for vehicles.</p></p>
