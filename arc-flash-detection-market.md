<p><p>Arc Flash Detection market Types include Fiber-Optic Sensor and Arc-Flash Relay. Fiber-Optic Sensors are used for detecting light and sending signals, providing a reliable method to measure the intensity of an arc flash event. They offer higher accuracy and faster response times compared to other sensor types. On the other hand, Arc-Flash Relays are devices that detect and mitigate arc flash incidents by detecting excessive light and current abnormalities. They act swiftly to trip the circuit breakers and limit the potential damage caused by an arc flash.</p></p>
