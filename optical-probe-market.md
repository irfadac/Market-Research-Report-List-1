<p><p>The optical probe market consists of different types of probes such as round, straight line, square, and rectangle probes. Round probes have a circular-shaped tip that is commonly used for general optical measurements. Straight line probes have a linear shape and are often employed for specific applications requiring precision. Square probes have a square-shaped tip and are suitable for measurements in square or rectangular areas. Rectangle probes have a rectangular-shaped tip and are commonly used for scanning or covering larger areas in optical measurements. These different shapes cater to a variety of measurement needs in the optical probe market.</p></p>
