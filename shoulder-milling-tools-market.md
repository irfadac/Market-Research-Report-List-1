<p><ul><li>General Shoulder Milling</li><li>Deep Shoulder Milling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shoulder milling tools are cutting tools used in machining processes to remove material from the workpiece. General shoulder milling refers to the process of removing material from the sides of a workpiece, resulting in a flat surface. This type of milling is widely used in various industries. On the other hand, deep shoulder milling involves removing material from a deeper area, often used in applications where a deeper pocket or groove is required. This particular type of milling is commonly used in the automotive and aerospace industries. Both general and deep shoulder milling tools are essential for precision machining operations.</p></p>
