<p><p>Large-format scanners are used to scan oversized documents, such as maps, blueprints, and artwork. There are two main types of large-format scanners: flatbed scanners and paper-fed scanners. Flatbed scanners have a flat glass surface where the document is placed for scanning. Paper-fed scanners, on the other hand, use a roll or sheet-fed mechanism to feed the document through the scanning process. Both types of scanners are widely used in industries such as architecture, engineering, and graphic design to digitize and archive large-format documents.</p></p>
