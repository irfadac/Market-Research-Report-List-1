<p><p>The side turn signal lamp market comprises two main types: Halogen Lamp and LED Lamp. On the other hand, LED lamps are more modern and energy-efficient. They use light-emitting diodes to produce light and offer advantages such as long lifespan, high brightness, and faster response time.
