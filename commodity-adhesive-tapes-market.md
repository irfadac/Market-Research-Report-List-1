<p><p>Commodity adhesive tapes can be categorized into various types based on the material used for their adhesive. The most common types include acrylic, rubber, silicone, and others such as EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) and butyl. Acrylic adhesive tapes offer excellent adhesion and durability, rubber adhesive tapes provide good flexibility and bonding on rough surfaces, while silicone adhesive tapes have high-temperature resistance. EVA and butyl adhesive tapes have specific applications such as sealing or insulation. Understanding the characteristics of these adhesive types helps in choosing the right commodity adhesive tape for specific industrial or commercial purposes.</p></p>
