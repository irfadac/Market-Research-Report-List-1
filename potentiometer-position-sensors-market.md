<p><p>Potentiometer position sensors are electronic devices used to measure the position or displacement of an object accurately. They consist of a resistive element, a wiper, and a fixed resistive track. As the object moves, the wiper changes its position on the fixed resistive track, thereby changing the resistance value, which is then converted into a voltage output. These sensors are commonly used in various applications such as automotive, aerospace, industrial, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The future outlook of the potentiometer position sensors market is expected to be positive due to the increasing demand for accurate position measurement in various industries.
